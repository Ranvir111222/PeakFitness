Our Aim Mission Fit India

Mission Fit India Points
  • Growing Awareness: Focus on health due to rising lifestyle diseases (obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular issues).
  • Government Initiative: Promotes physical activity, healthy eating, and overall wellness.
  • Historical Context: Builds on past campaigns like Swasth Bharat Abhiyan and Fit India Movement.
  • Objectives:
    • Promote daily physical activity and nutritious diets.
    • Improve access to recreational facilities.
    • Reduce lifestyle diseases (obesity, diabetes).
    • Empower communities to take proactive health steps.
  • Looking Ahead: Optimism for a healthier future with expanded initiatives and new partnerships.

Our Mission

At Muscle & Strength our mission is to build a platform that provides people with the knowledge, tools and products to reach their fitness and physique goals.
